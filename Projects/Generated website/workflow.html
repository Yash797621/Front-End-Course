Our Recruitment Workflow

1. Understanding Client Needs

We initiate the process by closely analyzing client requirements, organizational culture, and position expectations.

2. Strategic Sourcing

We source candidates through job boards, social platforms, headhunting, and our internal database.

3. Screening & Shortlisting

Each profile is screened against client criteria and shortlisted for further evaluation.

4. Candidate Evaluation

We assess shortlisted candidates on skills, experience, communication, and cultural fit.

5. Client-Candidate Coordination

We schedule interviews, collect feedback, and ensure smooth communication between both parties.

6. Turnaround Time

Our optimized processes ensure quick closures without compromising on quality.
