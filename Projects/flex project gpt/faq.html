Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to your most common queries.

What are your operating hours?

Our clinic operates from Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

We recommend booking an appointment to avoid waiting. However, we do accept walk-ins depending on availability.

We offer a wide range of services including cardiology, pediatrics, orthopedics, dermatology, and more.

Yes, we accept most major insurance providers. Please contact our support for verification before your visit.
